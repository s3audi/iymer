Founder "Fatih Poyraz"

Mr. Fatih Poyraz, originally from Trabzon,Caykara was born in 1976 in Uskudar,Istanbul.Graduated from Istanbul University,Faculty of Economics in English and got masters education on Economy Policy at the Social Sciences Institute at the same university.
Started his business life as foreign trade expert in a food production company. While His Excellency Recep Tayyip Erdogan was the Mayor of Istanbul Metropolitan Municipality,he joined the affiliate of the municipality, Hamidiye Natural Spring Water Company as Foreign Relations Coordinator and made the first export of Hamidiye Natural Spring water in its history. Served as General Manager in a Turkish-Uzbek joint investment project of a private logistics company in Tashkent,Uzbekistan..
Following his military service, worked as General Manager at an affiliate of Kartal Municipality. He also worked as Deputy General Manager in sharge of Corporate Relations,Marketing and Sales in a software company operating in energy sector. Served as Human Resources and Organizational Development Manager in a food production company and as Business Development Manager in a software company operating in defence and telecommunications sector.
He served as Project Officer at the Ministry of Interior,Directorate of Migration Management for the European Union Project : Budapest Process,Silk Routes Partnership.After, he worked as Migration Consultant and Arabic interpreter at Istanbul Provincial Directorate of Migration Management and Al Farah Children and Family Support Center which was a joint UNICEF and SGDD ASAM Project.
He served as regional expert in Africa Coordination Department of Turkish Maarif Foundation.
He took active role in different NGOs. Served as Deputy Secretary General at MUSIAD,Independant Industrialists and Businessmen Association.Founded UKDER-International Roots Development Solidarity and Cooperation Association.He is one of the founding members of ATI-Africa Turkey Initiative.
Married and father of three children.Speaks fluent English,Arabic,Uzbek, basic Russian,German an Kswahili.
